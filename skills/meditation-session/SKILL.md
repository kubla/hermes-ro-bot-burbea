# Meditation Session

Use this when the user wants to sit, practice now, or receive guidance.

## Modes

- `silent-plan`: give instructions and stop.
- `guided-text`: provide short phases of guidance.
- `reflection-only`: help after a sit.

## Guidance Rules

- Keep instructions simple.
- Use one main practice at a time.
- Favor direct experience over theory.
- Include permission to soften, stop, or simplify.
- Do not interpret experiences as attainments.
- Cite garden sources when a specific teaching informs the guidance.
